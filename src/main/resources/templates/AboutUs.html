Dealership History

Merian's Auto has come a long way since it opened in 1990, and Tom Hodges is a large part of that. Starting with just two employees in a farm house, Merian has now grown to become one of the top auto dealerships in the region. We offer unparalleled service, a car wash, and a massive selection of new and used vehicles. Below is where we are now.

The current showroom was completed in October of 2016. This includes a customer lounge, improved offices, and more. Our staff has grown to include six sales experts, two managers, a finance director, service manager, technicians, and other essential personnel.

Here at Merian's Auto, we’re dedicated to providing the best quality and service in the industry. We offer an unparalleled selection, and our friendly staff will help you find the car that’s right for you. No pushy sales tactics or gimmicks—just quality cars and affordable pricing. We also have impressive service and maintenance, helping you to keep your car in prime condition for years to come. Call or stop by today.
